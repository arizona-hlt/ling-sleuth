Introduction

By now you ought to have read through the case Picture Imperfect, which is already a solved murder mystery. We will be using the documents from this murder mystery to train our Authorship Detection System. What this means, is that will will configure our system to the point where we know that it can correctly identify the author of a document. At that point, we can apply the system that we have to our unsolved murder mystery documents, and correctly identify the author of the suicide note - the murderer.

Well how does one configure the Authorship Detection System? While there are some advanced techniques, we'll be focusing on the foundation and most critical part of the system - the features. What are the features? They tell the system the things to look for in the document, the characteristics about the author's writing to look at when determining authorship.

You've already learned a little about features and how frequency is used as a measuring tool when investigating these features.
