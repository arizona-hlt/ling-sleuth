The {{title}} feature: What is it?

A part of speech is what is commonly referred to as a grammatical category. You have probably heard of many of these, and may have been introduced to some of them during your syntax day at this camp. Some of the more common parts of speech include:

  • Common noun (NN)
  • Present tense verb (VB)
  • Adjective (JJ)
  • Determiner (DT)
  • Adverb (RB)
  • Modal (MD)

    • Each of these deserves a little explanation.
